Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about booking, cancellations, and more

Booking & Reservations

How do I book a flight on AeroVoyage?

Booking a flight is simple! Just follow these steps:

  1. Enter your departure and destination cities
  2. Select your travel dates
  3. Choose from available flights
  4. Enter passenger details
  5. Complete the payment process
  6. Receive your e-ticket via email

You can also book through our mobile app for a faster experience.

Can I book a flight without creating an account?

Yes, you can book as a guest, but we recommend creating an account because:

  • Manage all your bookings in one place
  • Access exclusive members-only deals
  • Faster checkout for future bookings
  • Earn reward points on every booking
  • Receive personalized travel recommendations
How do I know my booking is confirmed?

After successful payment, you'll receive:

  • A confirmation email with your e-ticket and booking details (check spam folder if not received)
  • SMS notification with your booking reference number

You can also:

  • View your booking in the 'My Trips' section if you're logged in
  • Verify your booking status on the airline's website using your PNR number
  • Contact our customer support for verification
Can I book flights for someone else?

Yes, you can book flights for others. Please ensure:

  • All passenger details are entered exactly as they appear on government-issued ID
  • For international flights, passport details must match exactly
  • You have permission to use the payment method
  • Contact information should be yours for booking updates
How far in advance can I book flights?

You can typically book flights:

  • Domestic flights: Up to 330 days in advance (varies by airline)
  • International flights: Up to 365 days in advance
  • Last-minute bookings: Up to 2 hours before departure (online) or at airport counter

Booking early often gets you better prices, especially for peak travel seasons.

Payments & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods for your convenience:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, RuPay
  • Net Banking: All major Indian banks
  • UPI: Any UPI ID through supported apps
  • Digital Wallets: Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay, Amazon Pay
  • EMI: Available through select banks (3-12 month options)

All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

Why does the final price differ from the initial price shown?

The total price includes several components:

  • Base Fare: The airline's basic ticket price
  • Taxes & Surcharges: Government taxes and airline-imposed fees including:
    • Passenger Service Fee (PSF)
    • User Development Fee (UDF)
    • Goods and Services Tax (GST)
    • Fuel Surcharge
  • Optional Services: Seat selection, meals, extra baggage etc. if selected

We display the full breakdown before you complete payment.

Is it safe to enter my payment details on your website?

Absolutely! We prioritize your security with:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment processing
  • Tokenization technology - we never store your full card details
  • 3D Secure authentication for card payments
  • Regular security audits by independent firms

For added security, we recommend:

  • Using UPI or wallets for faster, card-less payments
  • Enabling OTP-based authentication for your payment methods
  • Checking for the padlock icon in your browser address bar
Do you offer price matching or price drop protection?

Our pricing policies:

  • Price Matching: We automatically compare our prices with major competitors to ensure you get the best deal
  • Price Drop Protection: Available with our Premium membership - if the price drops after you book, we'll credit the difference as travel vouchers
  • Best Price Guarantee: Found a lower price elsewhere? Contact us within 24 hours of booking with proof and we'll match it plus give you 5% extra as credit
What currency are your prices displayed in?

All prices on our platform are displayed in Indian Rupees (₹).

For international flights:

  • Taxes and fees may be calculated in local currencies and converted to INR
  • Conversion rates are updated daily based on RBI reference rates
  • Your bank may apply additional foreign transaction fees

Cancellations & Refunds

How do I cancel my flight booking?

You can cancel your booking through:

  1. Website/App:
    • Log in to your account
    • Go to 'My Trips' section
    • Select the booking and choose 'Cancel'
    • Follow the prompts
  2. As Guest:
    • Use the 'Manage Booking' option on our homepage
    • Enter booking reference and last name
    • Proceed with cancellation
  3. Customer Support: Call +91 9876543210 for assistance

Cancellation fees depend on airline policies and fare rules.

How long does it take to process refunds?

Refund processing times:

Payment Method Processing Time
Credit/Debit Cards 7-14 business days
Net Banking 5-7 business days
UPI 3-5 business days
Digital Wallets 24-48 hours

Note: These are estimates and may vary based on your bank's processing times.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy depends on:

  • Airline Rules: Each airline has different policies for different fare types
  • Fare Type:
    • Non-refundable fares: May only get tax refunds
    • Flexible fares: Full or partial refunds available
    • Promotional fares: Often have strict cancellation rules
  • Timing of Cancellation:
    • More than 48 hours before departure: Lower fees
    • Within 48 hours: Higher fees or no refund

For exact cancellation charges, please check your specific fare rules or contact us.

Can I get a refund if my flight is delayed or cancelled?

According to DGCA regulations:

  • Flight Cancellation:
    • You're entitled to full refund or alternate flight
    • Compensation may apply if notified less than 2 weeks before departure
  • Flight Delays:
    • 2+ hours delay: Refreshments must be provided
    • 6+ hours delay: Option to cancel with full refund
    • 24+ hours delay: Compensation may apply

In all cases, contact the airline first at the airport for immediate assistance.

What happens if I miss my flight?

If you miss your flight:

  • No-show Policy: Most airlines consider this a forfeiture of the entire ticket
  • Possible Options:
    • Some airlines may allow rebooking with fees (often equal to ticket price)
    • You may be able to use the return portion if round-trip
    • Taxes might be refundable in some cases
  • Recommendations:
    • Arrive at least 2 hours before domestic flights
    • Arrive 3 hours before international flights
    • Contact airline immediately if you're running late

Check-in & Boarding

When can I check-in for my flight?

Check-in timelines vary by airline but generally:

  • Online Check-in:
    • Opens 48 hours before departure for most airlines
    • Closes 1-2 hours before departure
  • Airport Check-in:
    • Domestic: Closes 45-60 minutes before departure
    • International: Closes 60-90 minutes before departure

We recommend completing online check-in as soon as it opens to get better seat options.

What documents do I need for domestic flights in India?

For domestic travel within India, you need:

  • Mandatory:
    • Valid government-issued photo ID (any one):
      • Aadhaar Card
      • Passport
      • Voter ID
      • PAN Card
      • Driving License
    • Boarding pass (printed or mobile)
  • Recommended:
    • Printout or screenshot of e-ticket
    • COVID vaccination certificate (if required by destination)
What's the baggage allowance for domestic flights?

Baggage policies vary by airline but typical allowances are:

Airline Cabin Baggage Check-in Baggage
Air India 1 piece (7kg) 15-25kg
IndiGo 1 piece (7kg) 15-20kg
SpiceJet 1 piece (7kg) 15kg
Vistara 1 piece (7kg) + 1 personal item 15-30kg

Note: Always check your specific fare rules as allowances may vary.

Can I select my seat in advance?

Seat selection options:

  • Complimentary Selection:
    • Some airlines allow free selection during check-in
    • Basic economy fares often have random assignments
  • Paid Selection:
    • Most airlines offer paid seat selection (₹200-₹2000 depending on seat type)
    • Extra legroom seats cost more
    • Bulkhead/emergency exit seats may have restrictions
  • Recommendations:
    • Check-in early for better seat options
    • Families should select seats together when booking
    • Contact airline for special needs (wheelchair access, etc.)
What items are prohibited in cabin baggage?

Prohibited items include (but not limited to):

  • Sharp Objects: Knives, scissors (blades >6cm), razor blades
  • Liquids/Gels: Containers >100ml (must fit in 1 liter clear bag)
  • Flammable Items: Lighter fluids, paint, gasoline
  • Weapons: Firearms, ammunition, stun guns
  • Tools: Hammers, drills, crowbars
  • Other:
    • Self-defense sprays (pepper spray, etc.)
    • Lithium batteries >100Wh (with some exceptions)
    • Magnetic materials

For complete list, refer to BCAS (Bureau of Civil Aviation Security) guidelines.
