Refund Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2023

This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for obtaining refunds for flight bookings made through AeroVoyage India ("we", "us", or "our"). Please read carefully before making a booking.

Important: Refund eligibility and amounts are determined by airline policies and fare rules. This policy describes our processing of eligible refunds.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available under these circumstances:

  • Refundable fares: Full or partial refund minus fees
  • Flight cancellations by airline: Full refund including fees
  • Significant schedule changes: Option for refund if new schedule is unacceptable
  • Special circumstances: Medical emergencies or bereavement (with documentation)

2. Non-Refundable Bookings

For non-refundable fares:

  • Base fare is not refundable
  • Taxes and fees may be refundable
  • Some airlines may offer travel credits instead

3. Processing Timeline

Refunds typically follow this timeline:

  1. Request processing: 1-3 business days
  2. Airline approval: 3-5 business days
  3. Bank processing: 5-10 business days

Total time: 10-18 business days from request date.

4. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Navigate to "My Trips"
  3. Select the booking you wish to cancel
  4. Follow the refund instructions
  5. Receive confirmation email with refund details

For assistance, contact our Refund Department at refunds@aerovoyage.in.

5. Refund Methods

Refunds are issued to the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 7-14 business days
  • Net Banking: 5-7 business days
  • UPI: 3-5 business days
  • Digital Wallets: 24-48 hours

6. Contact Information

For refund-related inquiries:

Hours: Monday-Saturday, 9AM-6PM IST
